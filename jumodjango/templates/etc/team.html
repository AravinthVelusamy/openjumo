Our Team

The Jumo Team is a small group of engineers and nonprofit professionals committed to making the world a better place.

Chris Hughes
Founder and Executive Director

Chris co-founded Facebook in 2004 and helped lay the foundation for the communications and product management teams. Chris then directed online organizing for Barack Obama’s 2008 presidential campaign. He graduated from Harvard University with a bachelor’s in History and Literature.

Following: Freedom to Marry, GLAD, Partners in Health, Acumen Fund, Access, Innovations for Poverty Action

Kristen Titus
Managing Director

Kristen manages Jumo's fundraising, communications, outreach and operations and has helped to develop the organization’s mission and vision since its founding. Prior to joining Jumo, Kristen worked as a consultant to nonprofit organizations and cultural institutions. She is passionate about women’s rights and improving access to quality education and health care.

Following: Living Goods, Partners in Health, Creative Capital, GEMS, women’s rights, human trafficking, HIV/AIDS, girl’s education

Josh Hendler
Chief Technology Officer

Prior to Jumo, Josh was the Director of Technology at the DNC and Organizing for America. He's worked in a number of technology roles in political organizations and non-profits including John Kerry's 2004 campaign, the british Labour party, and Rock the Vote. Josh was also one of the early technical staff at Major League Baseball's MLB.com.

Following: Natural Resources Defense Council, Union of Concerned Scientists, Crohns & Colitis Foundation

Jen Chiou
Outreach Director

Jen works with the community to understand how Jumo can maximize our collective impact through connecting people with social issues, organizations, and opportunities to act. Previously, she worked at Teach For All and helped launch programs similar to Teach For America around the world. Jen was also a consultant at the Bridgespan Group and advised foundations and nonprofits. She has a BA from Stanford University.

Following: Population Services International, Teach For India, One Acre Fund, Village Reach, International Rescue Committee, human trafficking

Nick Thuesen
Web Developer

Prior to Jumo, Nick worked as a Senior Software Developer at The New York Times. He has an abnormal passion for hoodies and batman and is often seen drawing on tables at work.

Following: Union Of Concerned Scientists, Friday Initiative, Partners in Health

Brennan Moore
Developer and Designer

Brennan designs and develops much of what you see on the Jumo site. He likes Yerba Mate and is interested in building beautiful and useful tools that help people make more informed decisions.
