Our Privacy Policy

The Privacy Policy describes Jumo’s policies and procedures regarding the personal information and other data collected through the Jumo Site. By using the Jumo Site, you consent to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Information Collection and Use
  1. When you create a Jumo account, you will be asked to provide some personal information, such as your name, username, password and email address. Some of this information you provide during the registration process (such as your name, gender and the organizations that you follow) will be listed on the Jumo Site and accessible by the public.
  2. The Jumo Site is designed to connect individuals and mission-driven organizations and to share news, information and opinions to further charitable purposes. Except as otherwise specifically stated in this Privacy Policy, the information you provide to us via the Jumo Site will be made publicly available on the Jumo Site. Jumo will store metadata associated with the use of the Jumo Site and may share this information with various third parties, such as the organizations you follow and the Content with which you interact.
  3. To better improve our Content and the Jumo Site, we track some of the actions you take on the Jumo Site, such as “following” an organization or “liking” a post. Jumo may also track and retain the details of transactions or donation payments made on the Site.
  4. We use “cookie” technology to collect additional website usage data and to improve the Jumo Site. A cookie is a small data file that is transferred to your computer’s hard disk. Jumo may use cookies to better understand how you interact with our Content and improve the Jumo Site.
  5. Jumo uses a variety of services hosted by third parties to help provide the Jumo Site and to better understand the use of the Jumo Site. These services may collect information sent to your browser as part of a web page requeust, such as cookies or your IP request.
  6. Jumo uses certain third party social media web sites and services such as Facebook, on the Jumo Site. Jumo may collect information you have made available on these sites. In accessing these services through Jumo, you are sharing information with those sites, and the information you share will be governed by their privacy policies. You may be able to modify your privacy settings with these third party social media web sites.
Information Sharing and Disclosure
  1. We use the information we collect to make the Jumo Site available to our users, to measure the use of the Jumo Site, to improve the Jumo Site, and to provide you with customer support. We may share or disclose information for these purposes without prior consent.
  2. We may share or disclose your non-private, aggregated or otherwise non-personal information, such as your public posts or the number of users who clicked on a particular piece of Content.
  3. We may share transaction information with the trusted third parties responsible for such transactions, including donations made through Network for Good. The beneficiaries of said donations will be notified of your donation transaction.
  4. We may disclose your information if we believe that it is reasonable necessary to comply with a law, regulation or legal request; to protect the safety of any person; to address fraud, security or technical issues; or to protect Jumo’s rights or property.
  5. Registered users are encouraged to keep their information current and up to date. The Jumo Site provides users with the tools to access or modify personal information provided to Jumo via the Jumo Site.
Third Party Sites and Services

The terms of this Privacy Policy apply only to information that is collected through the Jumo Site.

The Jumo Site may contain links to third party sites and/or services, some of which may collect personal data and/or other information from users. Jumo is not in control of or responsible for the privacy practices of those third party sites and/or services. Users are advised to contact the third parties directly for information about their privacy policies.

Children

The Jumo Site is intended for users who are at least 13 years of age. Users who are under the age of 13 are not permitted to use the Jumo Site or provide information to the Jumo Site.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

The Jumo Site may change from time to time. As a result, Jumo may need to make changes to this Privacy Policy.

Jumo reserves the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy without prior notice. Your continued use of the Site after any changes or revisions to this Privacy Policy indicate your agreement with the terms. This Privacy Policy was last updated as of the date indicated above. Please check back and review this Privacy each time before you provide information to the Jumo Site.
