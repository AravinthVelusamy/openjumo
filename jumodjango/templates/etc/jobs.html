Want to change the world? Change it @Jumo

Why Jumo?

Want to make the world a better place? Excited about using technology to further social causes you care passionately about?

As part of the Jumo team, you have the opportunity to make an impact in the U.S. and across the globe on a whole host of urgent social issues, from maternal health in the developing world to unemployment in the United States.

Jumo connects everyday people to causes and organizations that are meaningful to them. We build smart social technology to help the people working to change the world gain more followers, supporters, volunteers, and donors.

Today, Jumo provides thousands of non-profits and NGOs social, dynamic presences on the web and has helped hundreds of thousands of people find and connect to organizations working in over 250 issue areas in regions around the world.

If you have that itching feeling that you want to do something more meaningful with your life, Jumo is the place for you.

Culture and workplace

Our office is a sunny loft in downtown Manhattan. We don't just work together here. We're a small group of friends and intellectual partners interested in developing solutions to social problems.

We value passion and thoughtfulness just as much as we do hard workers.

We're both a start-up and a non-profit organization. We have the heart of a charity, but with the urgency of your typical start-up.

Meet the Jumo Team

We're not your normal start-up, and we like it that way. Jumo was founded by Chris Hughes, co-founder of Facebook. Meet Chris, and the rest of the Jumo team.

Positions
Front End Developer
As a front-end developer at Jumo, you'll be:
  • Responsible for the overall design, guaranteeing cross-browser support and a high level of aesthetic integrity.
  • Writing reusable, modular code for the front-end layer of the Jumo website.
  • Concepting, prototyping and developing new functionality for Jumo.com
  • Advocating for a consistent, simple user experience.
You should:
  • Be passionate about world-changing and working to help non-profits and charities
  • Live and breathe HTML, CSS and Javascript
  • Have an understanding of good UI design. While it's not critical that you're a designer, you should be able to pull together prototypes, and have a familiarity with the concepts behind good usability.
  • Experience with Django and Python is a plus

Software Engineer
As an Engineer at Jumo, you'll be:
  • Building out the core of the Jumo platform
  • Prototyping and building new features
  • Working with the rest of the team to define the future of our product
  • Coordinating closely with our front-end developers, and systems engineers, and our outreach team
You should:
  • Be equally passionate about working to change the world and writing great software.
  • Have experience with a range of back-end and front-end technologies. This includes, but is not limited to Python (and the Django framework). You should know these technologies backwards and forward.
  • Familiarity with MySQL, other relational databases, and NoSQL data stores.
  • Have an understanding of various Agile methodologies and Test-Driven Development
  • Experience developing on iOS and Android is a plus but not necessary.
  • Have experience working with high-traffic web applications.
  • Be creative.

Analytics Manager
As an analytics manager at Jumo, you'll be:
  • Responsible for developing and implementing top-quality analytics, measurement and data strategies to inform our work, our offers and the user experience.
  • Integrate existing analytics tools to track user acquisition, retention and engagement
  • Work closely with both the non-technical and engineering teams to develop and implement valuable data analyses. This information should inform both Jumo's development life-cycles as well as the work of the organizations on Jumo
You should:
  • Live and breathe data
  • Have a talent for visualizing complexity
  • Be self-directed, and confident in searching for insights within large data sets
  • Have previous experience in analytics role or web development
  • Skilled in analyzing large, complex, multi-dimensional data sets, and an ability to create a compelling story with the data
  • Experience in creating insights and analysis for advertising and marketing campaigns
  • Demonstrated ability to manage multiple priorities, projects and deadlines
  • Be proficient in SQL, and have used a variety of statistical packages (including but not limited to R, SPSS, STATA)
